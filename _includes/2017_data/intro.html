The Welsh Premier League T20 is a dynamic, community-driven cricket league streamed live on Twitch, bringing together twelve teams each run by passionate members of the local cricketing community. This annual competition showcases grassroots talent and celebrates the spirit of Welsh cricket through fast-paced, entertaining matches.

Since its inception, the league has grown in popularity and prestige. The Cardiff Crocodiles claimed victory in both the 2023 and 2025 seasons, while the Bala Barbarians triumphed in 2024. Each season brings new rivalries, standout performances, and unforgettable moments.

The 2026 edition of the Welsh Premier League T20 is set to take place in early 2026, with the player draft anticipated before Christmas. Fans and players alike can look forward to another thrilling season of competitive cricket, community engagement, and digital broadcasting excellence.

Streaming & Community Engagement

  • All matches are streamed live on Twitch, allowing fans to follow the action from anywhere.
  • Each team is managed by a community member, fostering local pride and involvement.
  • Interactive features and live chats make the viewing experience engaging and inclusive.
